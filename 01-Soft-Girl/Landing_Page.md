# Soft Girl Aesthetic

![Soft Girl aesthetic example](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/7/7e/Pastel_aesthetic_example.jpg)

## Overview
The Soft Girl aesthetic is a popular style often associated with pastel colors, gentle fashion, and a cute, cozy vibe. 
It became especially popular through social media platforms like TikTok and Instagram.

## Key Characteristics

| Element | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Fashion | Cardigans, skirts, oversized sweaters |
| Colors | Pastels such as pink, lavender, and beige |
| Makeup | Light makeup, blush, natural looks |
| Mood | Cute, calm, and gentle |

## Why People Like It
Many people enjoy the Soft Girl aesthetic because it feels comforting and expressive. 
It allows individuals to present themselves in a soft, creative way without being overly bold.
