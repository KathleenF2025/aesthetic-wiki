# Dark Academia Fashion

## Overview
Dark Academia fashion is inspired by traditional academic clothing and vintage styles. 
It often reflects what students or scholars might wear in older European universities.

## Common Clothing Items
- Blazers and structured jackets
- Sweaters and cardigans
- Turtlenecks
- Plaid skirts or trousers
- Loafers, boots, or oxford shoes

## Color Palette
Dark Academia fashion usually focuses on muted, neutral tones such as:
- Brown
- Black
- Beige
- Dark green
- Gray

## Why This Style Fits the Aesthetic
The clothing style supports the Dark Academia theme by creating a serious, intellectual, and timeless look. 
It helps reinforce the idea of studying, reading, and appreciating classic literature and art.
