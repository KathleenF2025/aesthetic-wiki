# Dark Academia Aesthetic
![Dark Academia library](\/images/dark-academia.jpg)

![Dark Academia aesthetic example](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/4/4f/Old_library_dark_academia.jpg)

## Overview
Dark Academia is an aesthetic inspired by classic literature, old universities, and a love of learning. 
It often focuses on academic settings, vintage fashion, and a serious, intellectual atmosphere.

## Common Themes
- Reading classic books
- Studying in libraries
- Writing, art, and philosophy
- Romanticizing education

## Key Characteristics

| Element | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Fashion | Blazers, sweaters, scarves |
| Colors | Brown, black, dark green |
| Music | Classical or instrumental music |
| Mood | Scholarly, thoughtful, moody |

## Origins & Popularity
The Dark Academia aesthetic gained popularity online through Tumblr, Pinterest, and later TikTok. 
It appeals to people who enjoy academic environments and creative expression.
